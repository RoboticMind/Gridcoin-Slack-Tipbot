*Here Are the Steps To Get Started:*
1) run `/register` to register your account
2) run `/password add,[password]` to add a pasword (optional)
*To Add Coins Into Your Account:*
1) run `/deposit` then copy the address given by the command
2) open your wallet and click on the send tab
3) Then paste the addresss you copied into the section titled "Pay To"
4) In the section labeled amount, type an amount and then click send in the lower righthand corner
*To Withdraw Your Coins:*
1) Open your wallet and click on the recieve tab
2) Click new if you see nothing there
3) Select one of the listed addresses and click copy
4) run `/withdraw [Copied Address],[Amount of Gridcoin]["," ( Your Password |If You Set One| )]`
